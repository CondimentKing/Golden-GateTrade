About Golden Gate Trade

Golden Gate Trade was founded on a simple yet powerful idea: to make global trade accessible, efficient, and reliable for businesses of all sizes. Our journey began with Youcef Aissous and Zakaria Aissous — two seasoned professionals with over 20 years of combined experience in international logistics, customs compliance, and supply chain management.

What sets us apart is our commitment to transparency, precision, and long-term partnerships. We believe that successful trade isn't just about moving goods — it's about building trust across borders. Whether you're importing raw materials or exporting finished products, we offer tailored strategies to help your business thrive globally.

  • Experienced Leadership: Founded by industry veterans who have worked with Fortune 500 companies and government agencies.
  • End-to-End Solutions: From sourcing to delivery, we handle every step of the import/export process.
  • Customized Services: Every client has unique needs — we adapt our services accordingly.

Our Comprehensive Services

  • International Sourcing: Identify and vet suppliers from Asia, Europe, and beyond.
  • Freight Management: Full-service shipping via sea, air, rail, and road — including FCL/LCL options.
  • Customs Brokerage: Timely and accurate clearance through U.S. Customs and partner agencies abroad.
  • Trade Compliance: Ensure adherence to HS codes, export controls, sanctions, and documentation requirements.
  • Product Certification: Obtain necessary certifications such as CE, FDA, ISO, and country-specific approvals.
  • Supply Chain Consulting: Optimize your global operations for speed, cost, and scalability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need assistance with product classification?

Yes! Our experts can help classify your goods using the Harmonized System (HS) codes to ensure proper customs treatment.

How long does customs clearance typically take?

Clearance usually takes between 1–3 business days, depending on the complexity and completeness of documentation.

Can you help me find suppliers overseas?

Absolutely. We specialize in connecting clients with verified suppliers in Asia, Europe, and Latin America.

Are your services suitable for small businesses?

Definitely. We provide scalable solutions tailored to startups, SMEs, and enterprise-level clients alike.
